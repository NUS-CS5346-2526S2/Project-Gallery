Three forces reshaping our world

AI is simultaneously transforming the workforce, concentrating economic power, and drawing down planetary resources at a pace governments and communities are only beginning to grasp. Talent is migrating to a handful of wealthy nations. Infrastructure costs are quietly passing to households. Carbon and water demand are accelerating. The question is no longer whether AI has costs — it is who bears them, and whether that is fair.
